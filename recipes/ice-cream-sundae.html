Ice Cream Sundae Recipe

Description

I scream, you scream, we all scream for ICE CREAM!

A classic ice cream sundae recipe

Ingredients

  • Ice Cream
  • Flavored Syrup of Your Choice
  • Whipped Cream
  • Chopped Nuts
  • Cherry

Steps

  1. Add ice cream to a dish
  2. Drizzle the syrup over the ice cream
  3. Sprinkle nuts over the syrup
  4. Add whipped cream
  5. Put the cherry on top
  6. Enjoy!
