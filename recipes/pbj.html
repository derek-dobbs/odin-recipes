Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich Recipe

Description

This is a classic PBJ recipe that you will love!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Take a slice of bread and cover it with peanut butter
  2. Take another slice of bread and cover it with jelly
  3. Press the bread together with the peanut butter and jelly sides facing one another
  4. Enjoy!
