Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a quick and easy pizza recipe that you can make at home.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • Toppings of Your Choice

Steps

  1. Roll the dough into a circular or square shape
  2. Spread the sauce evenly across the dough as thickly as desired while leaving room for outer crust
  3. Spread the cheese evenly across the sauce
  4. Add your toppings
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 15 minutes
  6. Let the pizza cool for 5 minutes after removing from oven
  7. Cut into the desired number of slices and enjoy!
